Timeline JS Tutorial

Watch the following video to get an understanding of how to start working on your timeline after you have began to complie information about your Avatar. After each phase you will put your gathered information into your timeline. You will continue to do this after every phase of your ptoject.

Click here to begin your Timeline

Step 1: Go to "http://timeline.knightlab.com"

Step 2: Click on the "Make a Timeline" button.

Step 3: After the page automatically scrolls down click on the "Get the Spreadsheet Template" button to open a Google Sheets document.

Step 4: A new tab opens, click on the "Make a copy" button to create a new Google Sheets document.

Step 5: Input required data for your unique timeline.

Step 6: Once all data is inserted and complete click on "File", and then select "Publish to the web..."

Step 7: Click on the "Publish" button. A Google Drive pop-up should appear, click "OK" to publish.

Step 8: DO NOT copy the highlighted URL that is selected in the pop-up window. Instead, copy the URL from the top of the screen that is in the address bar.

Step 9: Head back to the timeline website and scroll down to step 3 on this webpage. Paste your recently copied URL into the "Google Spreadsheet URL" text box. Once you paste the URL hit 'enter' or click on a blank space on the screen.

Step 10: Scroll down to step 4 where you will copy the ENTIRE URL. Once you copy the URL, paste it in the address bar of a new tab in your browser.
